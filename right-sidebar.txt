										<h3>How To: Prevent Browning</h3>
										<p>For some of our ingredients, we include directions to cover cut produce with lemon juice, lime juice or vinegar to prevent browning.
										It's a way to keep your produce looking its absolute best.
										Certain fruits and vegetables (pears, apples and avocados especially) oxidize quickly when cut open.
										The natural acids (and vitamin C) in citrus juice and vinegar create a protective barrier between the produce and the air,
										keeping their appearance fresh throughout the cooking process.</p>
